Damage:
To beat Heat Man in Mega Man 2, equip the Bubble Lead weapon (obtained from Bubble Man), which is his primary weakness. The strategy involves a simple loop: shoot him with a bubble, jump over him when he turns into a fire form and charges, then turn and shoot him again, keeping your distance.


Avoiding Damage:
Heat Man’s movement pattern is predictable. He will shoot fireballs at you, and after being hit, he will turn into a flaming projectile and dash across the screen toward your position. As soon as he ignites, prepare to jump over him to avoid taking high damage.


Strategy:
If you lack the Bubble Lead, the next best weapons are the Atomic Fire (if fully charged) or simply using the default Mega Buster to wear him down. The fight is fast-paced, so prioritize dodging his charge over constantly shooting; his vulnerability increases when he stops to fire, giving you a clear opening.


How to get to Heat Man:
Before reaching Heat Man, you must navigate a challenging stage with disappearing blocks over lava. It is highly recommended to use the Item-2 jet sled (obtained from Air Man) to fly over the most difficult, complex disappearing block section. For the final section, you can also use Item-2 or carefully time jumps to avoid the Sniper Joe enemies.
