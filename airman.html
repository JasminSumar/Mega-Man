Preperation:
To defeat Air Man in Mega Man 2, it is highly recommended to tackle Wood Man first to obtain the Leaf Shield (or "Woodo Shield"), which is Air Man's primary weakness. The Leaf Shield is exceptionally effective because it not only deals significant damage to Air Man but also destroys or blocks the small tornadoes he sends flying across the screen, allowing you to bypass his hardest-to-dodge attack patterns.


Avoiding Damage and Battle Location:
Entering the boss room, maintain a moderately close distance and keep the Leaf Shield active, as it will act as a defensive barrier against the tornadoes and damage him upon contact. When Air Man jumps across the screen, try to stay on the opposite side to avoid contact damage, which is more punishing than the tornadoes. Keep in mind that he will fire tornadoes, then jump, then repeat this pattern until his energy is depleted.


Strategy:
If you are attempting this battle without the Leaf Shield, you can still win using the default Mega Buster. In this case, it is crucial to adopt an aggressive, damage-racing approach—stand close to him, keep shooting constantly, and mash the fire button, as Air Man has relatively low HP compared to other bosses and can be beaten through brute force. If you are fast, he will be defeated before his tornado barrage becomes too chaotic to navigate.


Tip:
During the fight, Air Man will create three tornadoes, which block your shots and push you back. Simply focus on jumping over them while keeping your fire rate high. After defeating him, you will be rewarded with the Air Shooter weapon (useful for Crash Man) and Item 2, which is a jet platform crucial for navigating later stages.
