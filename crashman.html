Damage/Strategy:
To beat Crash Man in Mega Man 2, your best strategy is to utilize his weakness, the Air Shooter (acquired from Air Man), which can defeat him in just 2-3 hits. When you enter the room, run directly toward him, but stop before you take contact damage. The Air Shooter sends tornadoes upwards, making it perfect for hitting Crash Man while he jumps. A highly effective technique is to stand close to him, wait for him to jump, and shoot the Air Shooter so all three tornadoes hit him simultaneously for maximum damage.


Alternate Strategy:
If you do not have the Air Shooter, you can still defeat him with the Mega Buster, though it requires precise timing and movement. Crash Man usually moves back and forth before jumping and throwing a time bomb. The crucial thing to know is that Crash Man will always jump immediately after you shoot him. You can use this behavior to control his movements, forcing him to jump over you while you maneuver to the opposite side of the room to avoid his bombs.


Avoiding Damage:
When dodging, move back and forth to prevent getting caught in the corner. Avoid standing directly under him when he jumps, as his Crash Bomber bombs stick to the floor and explode shortly after, dealing high damage. The best approach is to shoot once, jump over him or run under him as he jumps, wait for him to land, and repeat the process from the other side.


Tips:
If you are struggling, remember that you can collect energy in the hallway leading to his room to fill your weapons before the fight. If you have already used the Air Shooter to finish the level, the Mega Buster strategy is the only option, but it is manageable if you keep moving. Another alternative is to use the Leaf Shield if you have it, though Air Shooter is far more efficient, ending the fight quickly and making the fight much easier to handle.
