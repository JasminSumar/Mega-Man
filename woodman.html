Equiptment:
To defeat Wood Man in Mega Man 2, the most effective strategy is to use the Metal Blade, which is obtained by defeating Metal Man first. Wood Man is highly vulnerable to cutting weapons, and the Metal Blade allows you to deal significant damage while firing in any direction. If you do not have the Metal Blade, the Atomic Fire (Heat Man's weapon) or Air Shooter (Air Man's weapon) are also effective alternatives.


Damage:
The battle requires precise timing to avoid Wood Man's high-damage leaf shield. When he triggers his shield, he will throw it at you, and leaves will fall from the ceiling. Jump over the leaf shield as it approaches, and use the moment he is vulnerable—right after throwing the shield—to fire your weapons at him. He is often defeated in just a few hits with the Metal Blade.


Strategy:
Positioning is key to making the fight easier. Stay near the left or right edge of the room, as this gives you more time to react to the falling leaves. While he is jumping, you can run underneath him to reposition yourself, but be prepared for him to jump toward you repeatedly. Standing too close can make dodging the falling leaves difficult, so keep a moderate distance.


Strategy Alternative:
If you are only using the Mega Buster, it is still possible to win by keeping a consistent rhythm of jumping and firing. Focus entirely on avoiding the falling leaves, as they do substantial damage. With the right weapon (Metal Blade) and careful positioning, you can defeat Wood Man quickly without taking significant damage.
