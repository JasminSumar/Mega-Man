Strategy:
To beat Quick Man in Mega Man 2, the most effective strategy is to use the Time Stopper (obtained from Flash Man), which is his primary weakness. Upon entering the boss room, activate the Time Stopper immediately to freeze him in place, which will drain roughly half of his health bar without him being able to attack. Because the Time Stopper cannot completely eliminate him, you should wait until his health is already somewhat depleted, or use it to safely handle the first half of the fight before switching to other weapons.


Alternate Strategy:
If you run out of Time Stopper energy, Quick Man is heavily vulnerable to the Crash Bomber (from Crash Man), which inflicts massive damage upon detonation. Another effective, though more difficult, strategy involves using the Atomic Fire (from Heat Man) at full charge, which can remove half of his health in one hit. Alternatively, if you prefer a faster-firing weapon, the Air Shooter (from Air Man) is also effective against him.


Avoiding Damage:
Quick Man is extremely fast and jumps randomly, often throwing three boomerangs during his second jump. To avoid damage, stay near the edges of the room and jump over him when he dashes toward you. His boomerangs will pause after a moment and redirect themselves back toward your position, requiring you to be ready to jump or move again to avoid being hit from behind.


Survival:
The stage leading up to Quick Man is notoriously fast-paced, featuring insta-death force beams. To survive the stage, keep moving downwards, use the Item-1 platform to navigate tricky gaps, and bring along spare E-Tanks to restore health if you are hit by the beams. Saving your Time Stopper for the boss, rather than using it on the lasers, is crucial for winning the final fight.
