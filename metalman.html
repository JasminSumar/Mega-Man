Preperation:
Defeating Metal Man in Mega Man 2 is widely considered one of the best starting points in the game, as his stage is straightforward and his weapon is exceptionally powerful. To prepare for the battle, it is highly recommended to traverse the stage carefully, navigating the conveyor belts and watching out for the "Press" enemies that drop from the ceiling, which can be avoided by staying in place and moving only when they ascend. The boss fight itself takes place in a room with a conveyor belt floor that changes direction periodically, signaled by a brief screen flash.


Avoiding Damage:
Metal Man's AI is largely reactive, meaning he will jump and throw his sawblades, the Metal Blades, whenever you fire your weapon at him. To defeat him efficiently, the Quick Boomerang (acquired from Quick Man) is his official weakness, taking him out in four to seven hits. Alternatively, using the standard Mega Buster works well if you jump over his blades and shoot him in the air or when he is on the ground.


Strategy:
A key strategy is managing your position on the conveyor belt; if it moves you too close, Metal Man may jump over you to the other side. To avoid taking damage, jump when he fires his blades and keep your distance to make the trajectory easier to manage. Do not stay in one spot for too long, or he will eventually throw blades without provocation. Alternatively, the absolute best way to beat Metal Man, especially in a rematch, is using the Metal Blade weapon itself. Ironically, Metal Man is incredibly vulnerable to his own weapon, allowing you to defeat him in only one or two hits on normal difficulty. This makes him an excellent target for quick, high-damage attacks.


After Defeating:
Once defeated, you obtain the Metal Blade, arguably the most powerful weapon in the game, as it can be fired in eight directions and deals severe damage to many other Robot Masters, including Bubble Man, and even to Metal Man himself in later rematches. Taking down Metal Man first sets you up for a much smoother playthrough of the rest of the game.
