Preperation/Strategy:
To beat Flash Man in Mega Man 2, the most effective strategy is to use the Metal Blade weapon, which he is highly weak against. The fight takes place in a room with slippery, tiered ice platforms, where Flash Man will attempt to run into you or jump around before using his Time Stopper ability. By standing near a wall and spamming Metal Blades—even upwards—you can "jiggle" him in the air, preventing him from easily approaching you and killing him within 4 to 7 hits.


Alternate Strategy:
If you do not have the Metal Blade, the Crash Bomber (from Crash Man) is another strong weakness. Place the Crash Bomber on the wall near him, and he will often walk into the explosion while attempting to use his Time Stopper, resulting in massive damage. Alternatively, his pattern can be countered with the Atomic Fire (half-charged) weapon, or simply by using the standard Mega Buster, shooting him whenever he is not in his "Time Stop" frozen state.


Damage:
Flash Man’s main offensive move is to freeze Mega Man in place for roughly 2 seconds using his Time Stopper. During this freeze, he will fire a spread of shots in your direction. To dodge this, try to jump just before the screen flashes to move while you are still active, or be prepared to jump over his shots the moment you regain control.


Tips:
The fight in Wily's stage (rematch) is much easier because the ground is completely flat, making him easy to track with the Metal Blade. The best strategy overall is to enter the fight with full Metal Blade ammo and simply destroy him quickly before he has a chance to freeze you more than once. Defeating him rewards you with the Time Stopper weapon and Item-3.
